## Selected Approach: Industrial Brutalism meets Swiss Style

I have selected the **Industrial Brutalism meets Swiss Style** approach because it perfectly aligns with the "Operating System" concept. It conveys precision, technical authority, and a "no-fluff" attitude that appeals to both HVAC owners and PE firms. The "Mission Control" aesthetic requested in the brief fits naturally within this design language.

**Implementation Details:**
- **Font**: Space Mono for headers/data, Inter for body text.
- **Colors**: Dark background (#0f172a) with Safety Orange (#ea580c) accents and Steel Blue (#455A64) structural elements.
- **Visuals**: Visible grid lines, technical schematic icons, and data-heavy layouts.
